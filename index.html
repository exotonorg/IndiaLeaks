How to use IndiaLeaks

1. Download TOR Browser

Use of TOR browser is extremely important to guaranteeing anonymity while submitting information to IndiaLeaks. Read the following guide to download & install TOR.

Learn More

2. Open IndiaLeaks

Once TOR Browser is installed you can open this website again & click on the button below to access our secure submission platform or navigate to https://submit.indialeaks.org

Submit Now

3. (Optional) Use Tails OS

Use of Tails OS is advised in the most sensitive of disclosures. Proper use of Tails before submission will ensure no traces of data remain on your machine all the while provide extremely strong anonymity.
