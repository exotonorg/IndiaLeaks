Why use IndiaLeaks

1. Stay Anonymous

IndiaLeaks is built on top of Tor networking technology which ensures your identity remains a secret even to us. Read the following guide to download & install Tor.

2. Wide Media Coverage

We have teamed up with journalists from some of India's leading news organizations. This means any submissions you send us will have extremely wide media coverage.

3. Nonprofit & Open Source

IndiaLeaks is built on GlobaLeaks, an open source technology stack released under the AGPLv3 license by the Hermes Center for Transparency and Digital Human Rights.
